Coding Ninjas
Coding Ninjas is a prominent ed-tech company specializing in providing high-quality training in programming and technical skills. Founded in 2016 by Ankush Singla, Nidhi Agarwal, and Dhawal Parate, the organization offers a variety of online courses and bootcamps in programming languages, data structures, algorithms, web development, machine learning, and more. With a focus on practical learning and industry-relevant skills, Coding Ninjas aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Key features of Coding Ninjas include:

Comprehensive Curriculum: Courses are designed by industry experts and cover a wide range of topics from basic to advanced levels.
Experienced Faculty: Instructors are often industry professionals and alumni from top institutions like IITs and Stanford.
Mentorship and Support: Students have access to dedicated mentors who provide guidance and support throughout their learning journey.
Coding Practice Platforms: Tools like CodeStudio help students practice coding problems and prepare for competitive programming and interviews.
Job Assistance: Coding Ninjas offers placement support through its network of hiring partners and resume-building workshops.

Coding Ninjas Student Club at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur
The Coding Ninjas Student Club at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur, is a vibrant student-led organization that fosters a community of coding enthusiasts and aspiring software developers. The club aims to promote technical skills, collaborative learning, and innovation among students.

Objectives of the Club:
Skill Development: Organize workshops, coding bootcamps, and hackathons to help students enhance their programming skills and stay updated with the latest technologies.
Collaborative Learning: Foster a collaborative environment where students can share knowledge, work on projects together, and help each other improve.
Networking Opportunities: Provide a platform for students to connect with industry experts, alumni, and peers, facilitating mentorship and professional growth.
Competitive Programming: Encourage participation in coding competitions and contests to help students develop problem-solving skills and gain recognition.

Activities and Events:
Workshops and Seminars: Regular sessions on various topics such as web development, data structures, machine learning, and more, conducted by experienced professionals and club members.
Coding Competitions: Internal and inter-college coding contests to encourage competitive programming and reward top performers.
Hackathons: Events where students can work in teams to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems within a stipulated time frame.
Project Showcases: Opportunities for students to present their projects, receive feedback, and gain recognition for their work.
Mentorship Programs: Senior students and alumni provide guidance and support to juniors, helping them navigate their academic and career paths.
The Coding Ninjas Student Club at SRM IST Kattankulathur is dedicated to creating a supportive and dynamic community that empowers students to excel in the field of computer science and technology. Through its various initiatives, the club plays a crucial role in enhancing the technical capabilities and career prospects of its members.
